# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to making participation in the dompdf.js community respectful and constructive for everyone.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- being respectful in discussions and reviews
- giving technical feedback with clear reasoning
- assuming good intent and asking clarifying questions
- helping others reproduce and diagnose issues

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- intentionally disruptive behavior in issues, pull requests, or discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
